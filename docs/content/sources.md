## Verification Process

Sources are manually verified quarterly to ensure:

- **Authenticity** — Documents sourced from official channels
- **Currency** — Information remains current and accurate
- **Accessibility** — Links and references remain functional
- **Completeness** — Full context available for verification

### Verification Standards

**Government Sources:**

- Verified against official .gov.in domains
- Cross-referenced with official publications
- Direct contact with issuing agencies when needed

**Academic Sources:**

- Verified through institutional repositories
- Author credentials confirmed
- Peer review status documented

**NGO Reports:**

- Cross-referenced with organizational websites
- Methodology and data sources reviewed
- Author expertise validated
